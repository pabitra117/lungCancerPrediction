About Lung Cancer

What is Lung Cancer?

A type of cancer that begins in the lungs — the two spongy organs in your chest that take in oxygen and release carbon dioxide. It's the leading cause of cancer deaths worldwide.

Prevention

Avoiding smoking, reducing exposure to pollutants, eating a healthy diet rich in fruits and vegetables, and staying physically active can meaningfully lower your risk.

Early Detection

Regular screenings — especially low-dose CT scans for high-risk individuals — and awareness of persistent symptoms can lead to earlier diagnosis and far better outcomes.
